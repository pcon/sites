Coding & Random Projects

Quotes

In the #salesforce IRC channel, people can quote other users. The quotes are then stored, transferred to a couchDB backend (running on cloudant), then made viewable and searchable here.

Karma

In the #salesforce IRC channel, you can ++ or -- people or things and this is known as karma. This karma data is dumped nightly and pushed to a couchDB backend (running on cloudant). The karma app lets users see karma over time as well as the most recent "leaderboard" for top 10 karma spots

IRC Log

Since not all the people in the #salesforce IRC channel use clients that are always connected, I was requested to create a way for people to view what happened in the channel while they were gone. For this, data is collected via a Mozbot IRC bot and pushed to Cloudant every 30 minutes. The data is then made accessible and searchable via this web front-end
