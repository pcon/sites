Deadlypenguin Design and Photography is owned and operated by Patrick Connelly.

About

Patrick has been doing photography and design work since 1996. He fell into wedding and engagement photos after being asked by a friend to take their wedding photos. Since then Patrick has enjoyed working with people on their special day. Patrick's photography style is to try and bring out the natural personality of his subject by making them feel at ease.
