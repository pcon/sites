History

Last year a group of Salesforce fans traveled to Dreamforce in an RV.  We met in Chicago and traveled for three and a half days across the western half of this great nation.  Along the way we stopped in 7 cities and had meet-ups with local user groups.  We made new friends, answered Salesforce questions and ate local fair.

It was magical.

This year we’re putting that magic on the rails.

You may ask, “Why the train?”  Well, there are a several of reasons:

  • We can include a lot more people.  (The RV only held 8 folks.)
  • Amtrak will do the driving for us.
  • Traveling by train is greener than driving or flying!
  • If you can’t make the whole trip, we can pick you up along the way.
  • Trains are cool!

So, come with us on this journey. We guarantee* you’ll meet interesting people and see things you’ve never seen before.  And who knows, we could get mentioned in the keynote!

* Guarantee not valid in all states.
